Additional Info

Policies, accessibility, and brand guidance.

“NeoLearn Institute pursues recognized accreditation pathways and maintains compliance with applicable standards. We align policies with current regulatory requirements and publish updates transparently.”

“We practice privacy-by-design. Learners control their data with consent-first settings, clear retention windows, and export options. Security is enforced through encryption, role-based access, and continuous monitoring.”

“We strive to meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Our design system prioritizes color contrast, keyboard navigation, focus states, and screen-reader semantics. We welcome feedback to improve accessibility.”
